About us

Although my life was on the right track coming from my time in high school my biggest problem was finding a house. due to the fact that I had no person(broker) to guide me through what or which houses I would like to buy: all the decision making was thrown on me. I had no observation skills when it come to the quality of houses, so I ended up buying a crappy G+1 house that had little to no breathing area and, was a to some extent ugly: specifically the faint yellow paint of the exterior parts.

So i used my entrepreneurship skills to establish a broker organization that helps in many fields including realestate shopping. the errors of my choices was due to my lack of intel, now we help people communicate with large business organization in order for them to meet their wants and to prevent them from repeating the same mistake we did. We are here to you and for you, we serve and help. We ensure your desicions in house chosing will be one you won't regret!
